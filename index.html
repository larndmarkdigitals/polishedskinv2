How It Works

How healthy skin creates lasting results

Your skin is designed to repair, regenerate, and renew itself. At Polished Skin, our treatments support and enhance these natural processes to create healthier, stronger, more resilient skin.

Serving clients throughout Eugene, Oregon with customized age management and corrective skincare, treatments like ProCell microchanneling, Glo2Facial, Jet Plasma, and BioRePeel work by stimulating cellular renewal, improving circulation, increasing hydration, and supporting collagen and elastin production. These treatments help improve the appearance of fine lines, uneven texture, enlarged pores, laxity, redness, and dullness while strengthening overall skin health.

When controlled stimulation occurs within the skin, your body responds by producing new collagen and elastin — the essential building blocks responsible for firm, smooth, youthful-looking skin. This natural regenerative process helps restore vitality, improve texture, and create lasting results over time.

To further enhance results, we pair advanced treatments with professional-grade growth factors, peptides, antioxidants, and restorative ingredients that support healing and optimize skin function and results.

The result is age management and corrective skincare that creates healthier skin. Improved texture. Stronger barrier function. More even tone. Enhanced firmness. Lasting confidence.

Because healthy skin isn't created by damaging it. It's created by supporting how it naturally functions.
